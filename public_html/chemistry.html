SRL India — Global Chemical Leader

Founded in 1972, SRL (Sisco Research Laboratories) is one of India's largest manufacturers of laboratory chemicals, reagents and fine chemicals. With ISO 9001, ISO 14001 and ISO 17034 certifications, SRL supplies over 25,000 products to laboratories in 80+ countries worldwide.
